Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about LocalBotify and creating Discord bots.

What is LocalBotify?

LocalBotify is a comprehensive platform for creating, hosting, and managing Discord bots without any coding knowledge required. Our visual interface makes bot development accessible to everyone.

Do I need to know how to code?

No coding knowledge is required! LocalBotify provides a visual interface that allows you to create sophisticated Discord bots using pre-built components and templates.

Can I host my bot for free?

Yes! Our free plan includes unlimited local hosting. For cloud hosting with 24/7 uptime, consider upgrading to our Pro plan for just €5/ month.

What's the difference between local and cloud hosting?

Local hosting runs your bot on your computer, while cloud hosting runs it on our servers. Cloud hosting ensures your bot stays online 24/7 even when your computer is off.

How do I upgrade to the Pro plan?

You can upgrade to Pro directly from your dashboard. All your existing bots and settings will be preserved, and new features will be available immediately.

Can I transfer my bot from local to cloud hosting?

Absolutely! You can seamlessly transfer your locally hosted bots to our cloud infrastructure with just one click. All data and configurations are preserved during the transfer.
